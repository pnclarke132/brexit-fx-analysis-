# brexit-fx-analysis-
Brexit FX Impact Analysis (GBP/USD &amp; GBP/EUR)

## Overview
This project analyzes the impact of Brexit on British Pound Stering (GBP) rates relative to United States Dollars (USD) and Euros (EUR). I focused on analyzing the structural changes in exchange (FX) rates and volatilty before and after the 2016 referendum.

## Objectives
1. Evaluate how GBP FX rates fluctuated before, during, and after Brexit
2. Compare performances between GBP/USD and GBP/EUR
3. Assess changes in volatility and market stability

## Toolset
Power BI (data modeling & visualization)
Power Query (data cleaning & transformation)

## Key Insights
• GBP experienced a significant decline after the Brexit referendum in 2016, which has been sustained for the past decade
• The FX conversion rate declined was more pronounced against USD than EUR
• Volatility peaked before 2016, most likely due to the 2008 global financial crisis, but remained elevated after Brexit
• Post-Brexit FX rates stabilized at a lower level, signaling a structural shift in GBP valuation across the board

## Dashboard Features
• Interactive time-series visualization of GBP FX rates
• Period-based comparison (Pre-Brexit, Brexit Year, Post-Referendum, Post-Brexit)
• Volatility analysis using standard deviation
• Currency comparison across multiple metrics
